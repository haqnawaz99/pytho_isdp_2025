# Project Rubric (adopted from codex)

Use for Module 8 presentations and marking. Scale **1–4** per area.

## Scoring scale

| Score | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| 1 | Beginning |
| 2 | Developing |
| 3 | Secure |
| 4 | Strong |

## 1. Problem understanding

| 1 | 2 | 3 | 4 |
|---|---|---|---|
| Goal unclear | Partly clear | Clear and appropriate | Clear, useful, well explained |

## 2. Correct use of Python concepts

| 1 | 2 | 3 | 4 |
|---|---|---|---|
| Major errors; program stops | Some concepts correct | Most concepts correct | Confident and correct |

## 3. Code clarity

| 1 | 2 | 3 | 4 |
|---|---|---|---|
| Unclear names/structure | Some clarity | Mostly readable | Clear, consistent, easy to explain |

## 4. Testing

| 1 | 2 | 3 | 4 |
|---|---|---|---|
| Not tested | One test shown | Several tests | Multiple tests including unusual inputs |

## 5. Explanation (oral)

| 1 | 2 | 3 | 4 |
|---|---|---|---|
| Cannot explain | Explains some parts | Explains main logic | Clear and confident |

## Total guide

- **20–25:** Strong project  
- **15–19:** Pass  
- **Below 15:** Improve and resubmit one feature
